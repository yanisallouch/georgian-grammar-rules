In the Georgian language there is no grammatical category of gender

 

სტუდენტი ვარ - (I) am a student

მშიერი ვარ - (I) am hungry

 

In the Georgian language there is no grammatical category of gender. Therefore, the sentence like "I am a student" (სტუდენტი ვარ) can said by both male and female.

 

* There are no articles in the Georgian language.
