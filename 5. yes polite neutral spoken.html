“Yes”: დიახ (polite), კი (neutral), ხო (spoken)

 

ბატონო დავით, თქვენ ექიმი ხართ?

-

დიახ.

Mr David, are you a doctor?

-

Yes (polite).

 

სოფო, სტუდენტი ხარ?

-

კი.

Sopho, are you a student?

-

Yes (neutral).

 

ლუკა აქ არის?

-

ხო.

Is Luka here?

-

Yes (spoken).

 

In Georgian, consent can be expressed with the help of three words: დიახ, კი, ხო. In official speech or a polite conversation you need to use "დიახ" (polite "yes"). In conversation with friends or in an informal situation, you can use "კი" (neutral "yes") and "ხო" (spoken "yes").
