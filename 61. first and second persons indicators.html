1st and 2nd Persons indicators (მ, გ)

მე მ-ქვია

My name is (I’m called)

შენ გ-ქვია

Your name is (You’re called)

მე მ-აქვს

I have (inanimate)

შენ გ-აქვს

You have (inanimate)

მე მ-ყავს

I have (animate)

შენ გ-ყავს

You have (animate)

 

In case of type II verbs:

1st person indicator is the prefix -მ

2nd person indicator is the prefix -გ
