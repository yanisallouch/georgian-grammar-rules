Polite addressing

 

2nd Person, Plural

თქვენ ქართველები ხართ?

Are you Georgians?

თქვენ სტუდენტები ხართ?

Are you students?

Polite addressing

თქვენ ქართველი ხართ?

Are you Georgian?

თქვენ სტუდენტი ხართ?

Are you a student?

 

In Georgian, a personal pronoun in the 2nd Person Plural is used for polite addressing. Therefore, with the help of the pronoun თქვენ (you) not only several persons are addressed, but also one person, when there is a need for polite addressing.
