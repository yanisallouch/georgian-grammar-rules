Instrumental Case

 

Please read the table carefully:

 

ვინ? რა?

Who? What?

ვისით? რით?

By whom? By what?

Nouns with a stem that ends with a consonant

a computer   კომპიუტერ-ი

by a computer  კომპიუტერ-ით

a ball                       ბურთ-ი

by a ball                      ბურთ-ით

a boy                           ბიჭ-ი

by a boy                           ბიჭ-ით

Nouns with a stem that ends with a vowel (ა, ე)

a bag                           ჩანთა

by a bag                         ჩანთ-ით

a tree                                 ხე

by a tree                               ხ-ით

a day                              დღე

by a day                           დღ-ით

Nouns with a stem that ends with a vowel (ო,უ)

a girl                            გოგო

by a girl                        გოგო-თი

a violin                ვიოლინო

by a violin            ვიოლინო-თი

a creek                             რუ

by a creek                         რუ-თი

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

In case of the nouns with a stem that ends with a consonant (კომპიუტერი, ბურთი, ბიჭი...), the Instrumental case ending -ით is added directly to the stem (კომპიუტერით, ბურთით, ბიჭით...).

 

In case of the nouns with a stem that ends with a vowel -ა and -ე (ჩანთა, ხე, დღე...), the last vowel (ა, ე) is cut off before the Case ending (ჩანთით, დღით...).

In case of the nouns with a stem that ends with a vowel -უ and -ო (გოგო, ვიოლინო, რუ...), the Instrumental Case ending is -თი.
